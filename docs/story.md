# Quick Draw Story

The hero is a cosmic swordsman who begins in the upper atmosphere of a dying
world. Meteor fragments fall through the sky like a burning storm, blocking the
path to the stars.

Each slash cuts through the debris and pushes the swordsman higher. The journey
starts among clouds and blue atmosphere, then climbs through thinner air until
the sky breaks open into space. Beyond the meteor field waits a new planet: the
next destination, and the promise of survival.

The core fantasy is simple: break through the atmosphere, carve a path across
space, and reach a new world by cutting down every meteor in the way.
